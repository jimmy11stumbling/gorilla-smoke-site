Gorilla Smoke and Grill (South Zapata), located in Laredo, specializes in a variety of hearty dishes with a focus on health-conscious options. This restaurant offers a selection of tacos, including Tacos de Brisket and Tacos de Carnitas, alongside a range of appetizers such as Buffalo Fries and Onion Rings. Signature sandwiches like the Brisket Sandwich and Gorilla Burger are particularly popular among patrons.
